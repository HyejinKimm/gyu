STUDIO

GYU.

GYU is not Design. GYU is not Advertising. GYU is not Digital.

GYU is the space between traditional disciplines, we offer a fusion of crafts to cut through. We have no fear. We work across creative boundaries, mixing objectivity with experience and curiosity with expertise to solve communication problems. To us, the only discipline that really matters is excellence.

GYU is not a normal agency. We don’t like complexity, either in the work we make, or in the way we work. When we collaborate with like-minded clients, something incredible happens – we become friends. GYU exists to find a new path. For us, making game-changing work is a road best travelled together. If your mind is open, we will move mountains to work with you.
